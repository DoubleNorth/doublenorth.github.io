Task1

heading

  • proportion == percentage
  • males females == men women
  • from … to .. == between … and … == over a period of years
  • the number of + 可名复 / the amount of sth. + 不可名 == the figure for + 可名复 / 不可名
  • show, compare, illustrate(实在没有比较在用这个)

Weapon Warehouse

  • 上升 （前两个名词）

    • rise (rose), increase (increaesd), grow (grew), go up (went up), show a(an) upward(downward) trend

  • 下降 （都可以做动词 名词）

    • fall (fell), decrease (decreased), decline (declined, 绝对不能被动), drop (dropped)

  • 修饰

    • fall steadily， fall back to,

  • 介词

    • from … to …
    • by 表示幅度 数值、百分比都可以

  • 波动

    • fluctuate(d) between and 不要被动!

  • 保持平稳

    • remain stable(steady) at

  • 经历 变化

    • see (saw), experience(d), witness(ed)

  • 最值

    • peak at / reach its peak at
    • reach / fall to its lowest point at

  • 占多少份额

    • account(ed) for / make up / represent(ed) / constitute

  • 预测

    • is expected / predcited / projected to

  • 倍数关系

    • double, triple (n. adj.) has increased twofold (变成原来的两倍)
    • be twice (three times) as high/long/fast/popular as

  • 相对关系

    • is consistently higher/lower than…
    • The gap between … and … narrows(ed)…
    • exceed(ed) + 数字 百分数 / overtake + 对象

  • 程度 （急剧 稳定 持续）

    • sharp(ly) rapid(ly) dramatic(ally) – steady(steadily) gradual(ly)
    • consistent(ly)
    • significant(ly) / noticeable(ly) / considerable(ably) – slight(ly)

  • 大约

    • about, around, just over, just under

  • 分别

    • respectively
